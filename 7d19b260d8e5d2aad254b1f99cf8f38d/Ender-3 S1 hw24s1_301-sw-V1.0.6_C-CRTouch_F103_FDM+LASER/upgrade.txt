Note: In this update, it needs to update the mainboard firmware and screen firmware.   Mainboard Update & Instructions:  1. Move the "Ender-3 S1 hw24s1_301-sw-V1.0.6_C-CRTouch_F103_FDM+LASER.bin" file to the SD card.  2. Restart the 3D printer.  3. Wait until the update is finished.  Screen Update & Instructions:  1. Move a folder named "private" to the TF card.  2. Insert the TF card into the screen card slot.  3. Connect the screen cable.  4. Restart the 3D printer and wait until the update is finished.
